COVID-19 UPDATE

Effective Immediately

We realize that this current situation can be a stressful and scary time, and it's okay for us to admit that we are struggling. But we want to do our best to relieve any stress that we can, and so we've made a quick update for you!
As of today, May 24 2020, we are planning on having one last online service next week. We feel that even with the Stay at Home order lifted, we want to act out of love for everyone involved, and so rather than going back right away, we are choosing to wait until we feel it is safe to do so. We will still be trying to come up with some sort of a solution to involve those who do not feel safe attending at this point, and we will be announcing that on both this website and our Facebook page!
Thank you so much for your understanding and cooperation.
