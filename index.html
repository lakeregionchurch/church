Lake Region Mennonite Church

Detroit Lakes

Originally started in 1968, Lake Region Mennonite Church is a rural, globally-minded Mennonite Brethern church located just East of Detroit Lakes Minnesota on Highway 34, devoted to spreading the love of Christ to everyone and building connections within the greater church.

Something for Everyone

you are welcome here

Whether this is your first time ever going to church, or you've been a faithful church-attender your entire life, we can guarantee that you'll fit in. Who We Are

a bible based community

So maybe you're wondering a little about who we are? Well, we'd love to tell you. With our congergation being made up of mostly farmers and loggers from rural Minnesota, we're nothing more than a bunch of broken and hurting people trying to love God and love people. You are welcome here and will fit right in, no matter what you look like or who you are. God's love is for everyone, and that includes you.
