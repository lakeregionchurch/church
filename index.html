COVID-19 UPDATE

Effective Immediately

As many may know, our world is currently facing a pandemic called Coronavirus, or COVID-19, which is quite dangerous for the eldery and people with pre-existing health conditions.
We believe that above all else, Jesus loves us and has not given us a spirit of fear, but rather one of strength and power. However, we also are given a sound mind and the gift of being able to make logical and morally sound choices. Because of that, we will not be holding services in person, until further notice.
During this period of uncertainty, we would ask you to join us in seeking God with so many of our distractions removed, as well as joining us at 10:30am on Sunday mornings for our livestream on YouTube!
