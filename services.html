When and Where

we want to see you

When, where, and how to show up can all be both a little confusing and a little intimidating- especially when you're visiting a new church. That's why we've put together this handy-dandy super helpful guide to make your time as easy as possible!

Firstly, when to show up. This depends on what time of year you plan on coming! If you're coming during the school year (September - May) then you can either come at 9:30 AM for a Small group study time or at 10:30 AM for our regular service. However, if you're coming between June and August, then you can show up as early as 9:30 for light snacks, and then we'll start a more casual service at 10!

Next, where to show up. This is really easy. Like super. We're located at 35502 State Highway 34 East, Detroit Lakes, MN 56501. Just throw that address into your GPS and you're golden.

Finally, how to show up. This sometimes can be the most stressful part; however, we don't want it to be stressful for you. Wear whatever you feel comfortable with and we'll accept you all the same.
